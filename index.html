Comparative MOS Experiment

Evaluate the quality of speech synthesis

Thank you for participating in this experiment!

In this experiment, we use a computer to synthesize human speech. You are asked to listen and compare how natural (i.e. human sounding) is the second recording compared to the first? Please focus on the naturalness of prosody and rythm (pitch, energy, ...) and audio quality (noise, sound clarity, timbre, ...).
If the fist audio sound more natural, your score should be negative, and vice versa.

  • 3（Much better)
  • 2（Better)
  • 1（Slightly better)
  • 0（About the same)
  • -1（Slightly worse)
  • -2（Worse)
  • -3（Much worse)
Note that the order of the audios appeared in each question is randomized.
